# Project Repository: Reports & Presentations

This repository contains the list of reports of projects created as well as their presentations. 

The projects include a diverse range of topics within the domain of economics:

1. **The empirical understanding of consumer behaviour on acceptance of technology (CBDC)**
   * *Methodology/Focus:* Using TAM and UTAUT model innovation

2. **Creating a Risk Aversion Index using the RBI speeches and MPC meetings minutes for the economy to understand and predict market behaviour**
   * *Methodology/Focus:* A Transfer Learning using FinBERT and Dictionary Based Approach
   * *Status:* ⚠️ **Ongoing!**

3. **Using the novel approach of ICA+VECM to understand the transmisson of monetray policy in India.**

4. **Empirical study to understand the factors affecting the happines index of 170 countries over the period of 5 years**
   * *Methodology/Focus:* Exploratory Data Analysis

5. **Task-Based Approach to understand the impact of AI on different sectors of Indian Economy**
   * *Methodology/Focus:* A DiD and Fixed Effects approach

6. **Analysis of Solar Policy dissemination across all the Indian States**
   * *Methodology/Focus:* Bootstrap-DEA approach and SW-2 Algorithm
